Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Teleprompter ("the App"), developed by tpmt.dev, handles your information. Teleprompter is a remote session controller for Claude Code that connects to a daemon service via an encrypted relay.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to any server. All session data remains end-to-end encrypted and is never accessible to us.

Camera

The App uses your device camera solely to scan QR codes during device pairing. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or processed beyond the QR code content.

Microphone

The App uses your device microphone for voice commands to control Claude Code sessions. Audio is streamed directly to the OpenAI Realtime API for processing and is not stored by the App. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

End-to-End Encryption

All communication between the App and your daemon is end-to-end encrypted using X25519 key exchange and XChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption. Pairing keys are exchanged offline via QR code. Relay servers only forward ciphertext and cannot decrypt any content.

Local Storage

Pairing keys and connection settings are stored locally on your device using platform-provided secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android, browser storage on web). No account or login is required.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI Realtime API — Used for voice command processing when the voice feature is active.
  • Expo Updates — Used for over-the-air app updates. May collect basic device information as described in Expo's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

No user data is collected on our servers. Relay servers hold only the most recent encrypted frames (up to 10 per session) transiently in memory for delivery purposes. These are discarded when the session ends.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or user tracking mechanisms.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
